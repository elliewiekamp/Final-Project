OC Dirtbags Baseball Academy

Our Objective:

This academy is going to allow the elite baseball player of the younger generation the opportunity to learn the basic fundamentals, both physically and mentally, in order to play the game at the competitive level of high school and beyond. Our objective is for every player to leave this academy with the understanding of how to properly play the game, while learning everyday life lessons in the process.
 

“To Win A Championship You Don't Need To Be The Best Team In Your League, City, State Or Country ~ Just The Better Team Each Time You Play!”
